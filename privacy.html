Privacy Policy - SinaluzPipeline

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

SinaluzPipeline ("we", "our", or "the Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our automated video publishing platform.

2. Information We Collect

SinaluzPipeline collects only the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authenticate with TikTok, we receive basic account information (such as your TikTok open ID and display name) solely to verify your identity and associate uploads with your account.
  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens provided by TikTok are used temporarily to authenticate API requests. These tokens are not stored permanently.

We do not collect passwords, payment information, location data, or any other personal data beyond what is described above.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account via the official TikTok Login Kit.
  • Upload video content as drafts to your TikTok account via the Content Posting API.

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the core functionality of the Application.

4. TikTok API Data Usage

SinaluzPipeline uses the following TikTok API scopes:

  • user.info.basic: To verify the authenticated TikTok account before uploading content.
  • video.upload: To upload video files as drafts to your TikTok account for your review and publication.

Data obtained through TikTok's API is used only for the purposes stated above and in accordance with TikTok's API Developer Policies.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. No user data is transferred to external services, advertisers, or partners.

6. Data Retention

SinaluzPipeline does not retain personal data beyond the duration of an active session. OAuth tokens are used in real-time and are not stored in any database or persistent storage.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information during transmission. All API communications with TikTok are conducted over HTTPS.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the Application's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings.
  • Request information about what data, if any, is associated with your account.
  • Request deletion of any stored data associated with your account.

To revoke access, go to TikTok → Settings → Security → Connected Apps and remove SinaluzPipeline.

9. Children's Privacy

SinaluzPipeline is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository: https://github.com/testes-app/-sinaluz-legal
